Schoolvoice comes with advanced and feature-rich school communication features designed to enhance and school-parent engagement. Here are some features of Schoolvoice that make it one of the top Edmodo alternatives.

Actionable Messages

A unique feature of Schoolvoice which enables schools and teachers to message parents with predefined built-in responses. The Actionable Messages can be used to send school announcements, circulars, request school fee payments, request documents, and much more.

Teacher Drive

Free cloud storage for teachers to store school files and documents. With the Teachers Drive, teachers can share class materials, share homework, score sheets, and other school documents with students and parents to access them from the comfort of their homes.

Rewards and Challenges

This feature lets teachers conduct fun class activities and challenges and reward students with trophies for their performance, positive behaviors, and skills.

Live Broadcasts

Enables teachers to go live from a class and interact with students and parents to make education and parent-teacher interaction possible from anywhere.

Instant Reports

A feature that measures parent engagement and provides school administrators deep insights into how communication is happening in the platform to make the right decisions to improve school activities.

Emergency Messaging

An emergency feature that schools and teachers can use to reach parents quickly when immediate parental intervention is required. The Emergency Messaging feature sends out alerts to the parent's smart devices even if the device is silent or without internet.
